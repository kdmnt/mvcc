1. paste the 4 files in /home/dbtech/.local/share/applications/:
executemvcc<dbms>.desktop

<Filename>executemvcc<dbms-of-choice>.desktop</Filename>

3. open a terminal and input:
xfdesktop --reload

sudo apt install libatk-adaptor
